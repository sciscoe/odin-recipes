Mangosteen Berry Bliss

Description

This colorful smoothie is not only delicious, it also packs an antioxidant punch to help boost your health and give your body the fuel it needs to battle your day. Thanks to the tropical superfood Mangosteen, it also has powerful anti-inflammatory and anti-cancer benefits.

The papaya in this recipe is rich in prebiotic fiber, making this smoothie a wonderful choice to help nourish the good bacteria in your gut that digests your food and keeps you healthy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh papaya
  • 2 fresh mangosteen fruits (remove the shells + seeds)
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries
  • 1/2c up frozen cherries
  • Splash of water
  • Handful of ice

Steps

  1. Cut fresh fruits into large chunks.
  2. Add all fresh and frozen fruits to the blender with a splash of water.
  3. Pulse the blender a few times to begin mixing.
  4. Add a small amount of ice.
  5. Blend on high, stopping to add more ice as necessary to achieve your desired consistency.
  6. Pour into your favorite glass, and enjoy!
