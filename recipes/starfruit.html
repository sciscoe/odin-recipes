Shine Bright Like A Starfruit

Description

This delicious green smoothie is not only rich in antioxidants, it also has powerful cognitive benefits provided by the superfood Matcha. Matcha is a nutrient rich powdered green tea that contains caffeine and L-Theanine which gives an energy boost without all the jitters that coffee can give.

The kiwi and starfruit in this recipe are full of vitamin C which has been been proven to aid immune responses, as well as improve skin health. Enjoy this fruity, creamy concoction, knowing it is helping both your body and mind :)

Ingredients

  • 2 Bartlett Pears, seeds and stem removed
  • 2 Whole Starfruits, seeds removed
  • 1 Kiwi, peeled
  • 1/2 cup Vanilla almond milk
  • 1 tsp Matcha powder
  • Handful of ice

Steps

  1. Cut fresh fruits into large chunks.
  2. Add all fruits to the blender with Matcha powder.
  3. Pour almond milk on top.
  4. Pulse the blender a few times to begin mixing.
  5. Add a small amount of ice.
  6. Blend on high, stopping to add more ice as necessary to achieve your desired consistency.
  7. Pour into your favorite glass, and enjoy!
