Summer Hydration Salvation

Description

This berry-licious smoothie is the perfect way to quench your thirst in the summer heat. Made with both watermelon and coconut water which contains natural electrolytes, it effectively hydrates the body while also pumping your system full of antioxidants that help fight free radicals.

The mint and lemon provide an earthy, herbacious flavor while cutting the sweetness of the coconut and berries. Enjoy this as a slurp-able smoothie, or add extra ice to turn it into a satisfying natural slushie bowl.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Blueberries
  • 1 cup Strawberries
  • 1 1/2 cups Watermelon
  • 1 Kale leaf
  • 7 Mint leaves
  • Coconut water to thin
  • 1/4 Lemon, juice
  • Handful of ice

Steps

  1. Cut watermelon into large chunks.
  2. Add all fruits, kale, and mint to the blender with squeezed lemon juice.
  3. Pour coconut water on top.
  4. Pulse the blender a few times to begin mixing.
  5. Add a small amount of ice.
  6. Blend on high, stopping to add more ice as necessary to achieve your desired consistency.
  7. Pour into your favorite glass, and enjoy!
